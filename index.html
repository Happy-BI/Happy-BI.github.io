Biography

Huan Bi is a PhD student at the University of Electrical and Communications. His research interests include the preparation of high-efficiency perovskite solar cells, including single-junction cells and tandem cells, with a focus on lead-free perovskite solar celle. In addition, he also studies polymers and electrospinning. He has published many high-level research papers and serves as a reviewer for some journals.
